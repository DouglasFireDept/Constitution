## Article XII - Excused Absences

### Section 1: Acceptable Excuses

The sickness of the member or in the memberâ€™s family, indispensable business, members vocation, or vacation; shall be sufficient excuse of non-attendance. An oral excuse must be presented in advance of the meeting to be missed. Such excuse shall be made to an officer of the department. That officer must then tell the secretary-treasurer so accurate records can be made.

### Section 2: Leave of Absence

The chief, or assistant chief, may grant leave of absence at their discretion to any requesting member.
