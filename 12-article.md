## Article XII - Excused Absences

### Section 1: Acceptable Excuses

Sufficient excuse of non-attendance includes but is not limited to the sickness of the member or in the memberâ€™s family, indispensable business, vocation, vacation, or fire related training. A text message, email, or oral excuse must be presented in advance of the meeting to be missed. Such excuse shall be made to an officer of the department. That officer must then tell the secretary-treasurer so accurate records can be made.

### Section 2: Leave of Absence

The chief, or assistant chief, may grant leave of absence at their discretion to any requesting member.
